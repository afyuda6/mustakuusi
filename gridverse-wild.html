💣 Gridverse: Wild — Logic Meets Danger!

Dive into Gridverse: Wild, a minimalist twist on the classic Minesweeper — designed for sharp minds and careful clickers.

Every tap counts. Every move could reveal a treasure or trigger a trap. Can you clear the grid without hitting the hidden hazards?

🎮 Game Features:

  • 🧠 Minesweeper Logic — Reveal cells, avoid hidden traps, and use numbers to deduce safe spots.
  • ⏱️ Timed Challenge — Test your speed and precision under pressure.
  • 💀 No Hints, No Safety Net — One wrong click could end your run. Win with pure strategy.
  • 🏆 Global Leaderboard — Compete with players worldwide via Google Play Games.
  • 🌌 Minimalist Visuals — Focus on the grid, not the clutter.
  • 📴 Offline Mode — Play anywhere, anytime, without an internet connection.

Whether you’re a Minesweeper veteran or a new challenger seeking tactical thrill, Gridverse: Wild delivers a clean, intense puzzle experience — one grid at a time.
