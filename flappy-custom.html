Flappy Custom

Jump, flap, and beat your own high score!

About the Game

Flappy Custom is a fast-paced, tap-to-fly game inspired by the classic Flappy Bird. Navigate your character through custom-themed obstacles and try to survive as long as you can!

Game Features

  • Endless flappy gameplay with custom characters
  • Global leaderboard with Google Play Games
  • Unlockable skins and achievements
  • Offline mode supported

Why We Request Google Account Access

Flappy Custom may request access to your Google account to:

  • Authenticate your identity for leaderboard and achievements
  • Save and sync your high scores and progress
  • Display your username and profile picture on the leaderboard

We value your privacy. Your data is used only within the game and never shared or sold.
