Flappy Custom

Fly through pipes in a classic-style flappy game — now with a twist: customization!

Flappy Custom is a fast-paced, tap-to-flap arcade adventure inspired by the legendary flappy game. Take to the skies with a character that’s uniquely yours, navigate custom-themed obstacles, and aim for the highest score.

🎮 Game Features:

  • 🚀 Simple tap-to-flap gameplay that’s easy to pick up, hard to master
  • 🎨 Customizable characters with unlockable skins
  • 🏆 Global leaderboard & achievements via Google Play Games
  • 🌈 Smooth visuals and fun animations
  • 📴 Offline mode supported

Whether you're a casual gamer or a flappy veteran, Flappy Custom delivers fast, fun, and frustratingly addictive gameplay.

🔐 Why We Request Google Account Access:

Flappy Custom may ask for access to your Google account to:

  • Authenticate your identity for leaderboard and achievements
  • Save and sync your high scores and progress
  • Display your username and profile picture on the leaderboard

We value your privacy. Your data is used only within the game and never shared or sold.
