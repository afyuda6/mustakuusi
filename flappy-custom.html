🕹️ Flappy Custom — Tap, Fly & Customize!

Flappy Custom is a fast-paced arcade flying game inspired by the legendary flappy challenge — but with a creative twist! Tap to flap, dodge obstacles, and soar through colorful skies with a character that’s uniquely yours.

🎮 Game Features:

  • 🚀 Classic Tap-to-Flap Gameplay — Easy to learn, hard to master.
  • 🎨 Customizable Characters — Unlock fun skins and fly in style.
  • 🌈 Smooth Visuals — Clean graphics and satisfying animations.
  • 🏆 Global Leaderboard & Achievements — Compete with friends worldwide via Google Play Games.
  • 📴 Offline Mode — Play anywhere, anytime, no internet required.

Whether you’re a casual player or a flappy veteran, Flappy Custom delivers that classic tap-to-fly thrill you love — now with custom skins and global competition.
