Privacy Policy for Buttons 4: Owl & Hippo

Last updated: August 15, 2025

Buttons 4: Owl & Hippo is a mobile game developed by mustakuusi. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our app.

Information Collection and Use

Our app itself does not directly collect personally identifiable information. However, we use third-party services that may automatically collect certain information to provide advertisements, analytics, and game features, including:

  • User IDs (via Google Play Games Services)
  • Device identifiers / Advertising ID (via Unity Ads)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics (via Google Play Games and Unity Ads SDKs)

Why This Data is Collected and Shared

These data are collected and shared for the following purposes:

  • App functionality: To enable sign-in, achievements, leaderboards, and other game features.
  • Analytics: To monitor app performance, diagnose and fix bugs, and improve user experience.
  • Advertising or marketing: To serve and measure advertisements via Unity Ads.
  • Fraud prevention, security, and compliance: To detect fraud and ensure secure operation of the app.
  • Account management: To manage user accounts through Google Play Games Services.

Data Encryption

All data transmitted to third-party services is encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS protocols.

Advertising and Children

Our app uses Unity Ads to show advertisements. When the app is used by children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your country), Unity Ads will only display non-behavioral ads, in compliance with Google Play’s Families Policy. Advertising ID is used for ad measurement, frequency capping, and non-personalized ad serving, and it is not used to personally identify users.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services, which may collect and use data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Unity Ads (for advertisements) – Unity Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Games Services (for achievements, leaderboards, and cloud saves) – Google Privacy Policy

Opt-Out and Settings

Users can manage or opt out of certain data collection:

  • Unity Ads: Follow instructions on the Unity Privacy Policy page to manage ad preferences or opt out of personalized ads.
  • Google Play Games: Sign out or manage permissions in your Google account settings.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your country). If you believe we have collected personal data from a child, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove it.

Required Data Collection

The collection of the data listed above is required for the app to function properly. Users cannot disable this collection within the app.
