We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly. However, our app uses third-party services that may collect certain data automatically for advertising, analytics, and game features, such as:

The app uses the following third-party services:

These services may collect and use data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

How We Use Data

Data collected by these services may be used to:

Opt-Out

Users can manage or opt out of data collection by adjusting their settings:

  • Ads: Go to Settings > Google > Ads and enable Opt out of Ads Personalization
